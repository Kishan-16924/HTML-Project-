Welcome To The Humburger Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound ground beef (preferably 80% lean, 20% fat)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 4 hamburger buns
  • Your favorite toppings (lettuce, tomato, onion, cheese, pickles, etc.)
  • Condiments of your choice (ketchup, mustard, mayonnaise)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your grill or stovetop pan to medium-high heat.
  2. Divide the ground beef into 4 equal portions and shape them into patties. Season each patty with salt and pepper on both sides.
  3. Place the patties on the preheated grill or pan and cook for about 4-5 minutes per side for medium doneness. Adjust the cooking time based on your preference for doneness.
  4. In the last minute of cooking, add a slice of cheese on each patty, if desired, and cover the grill or pan to melt the cheese.
  5. While the patties are cooking, lightly toast the hamburger buns on the grill or in a toaster.
  6. Assemble the hamburgers by placing the cooked patties on the bottom half of each bun.
  7. Add your favorite toppings such as lettuce, tomato, onion, and pickles on top of the patties.
  8. Spread condiments like ketchup, mustard, and mayonnaise on the top half of the buns.
  9. Place the top half of the bun over the toppings to complete the burgers.
  10. Serve the hamburgers hot and enjoy!
