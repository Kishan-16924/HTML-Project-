Welcome To The Pancake Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 3/4 cup buttermilk
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter, melted
  • Cooking spray or additional butter for greasing the pan
  • Maple syrup or toppings of your choice

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
  2. In a separate bowl, whisk together the buttermilk, milk, egg, and melted butter.
  3. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and stir until just combined. The batter may be slightly lumpy.
  4. Heat a griddle or non-stick skillet over medium heat and lightly coat it with cooking spray or butter.
  5. Pour 1/4 cup of batter onto the griddle for each pancake. Cook until bubbles form on the surface, then flip and cook the other side until golden brown.
  6. Repeat the process with the remaining batter.
  7. Remove the pancakes from the griddle and stack them on a serving plate.
  8. Optional: Top the pancakes with maple syrup, fresh fruit, whipped cream, or other toppings of your choice.
  9. Serve the pancakes warm and enjoy!
